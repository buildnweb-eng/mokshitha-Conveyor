# Video Assets

## Hero Video Setup

To add your conveyor belt video to the homepage:

1. **Prepare your video:**
   - Duration: ~10 seconds (looping)
   - Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
   - Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
   - File size: Keep under 5MB for fast loading

2. **Add the video:**
   - Place your video file here as: `hero-conveyor.mp4`
   - The video will autoplay, loop, and be muted (required for autoplay)

3. **Fallback:**
   - If no video is added, the poster image will display
   - The site works perfectly with or without the video

## Video Optimization Tips

- Compress your video using tools like HandBrake or FFmpeg
- Use H.264 codec for best browser compatibility
- Consider creating a WebM version for better compression
- Test loading times on mobile devices
